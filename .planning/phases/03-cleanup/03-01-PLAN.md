<objective>
Retain historical transport columns while making `pty-event-stream` the only active transport that can be written or advertised to Relay clients.
</objective>

<threat_model>
## Threat Model

| Threat | Severity | Mitigation |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Historical `transport='tmux'` rows regain operational meaning during auth work | high | Keep legacy reads explicit, normalize legacy rows to `lost`, and prevent active writes of new tmux sessions. |
| Relay metadata advertises tmux as remotely controllable | medium | Narrow `RelaySession.transport` to `'pty-event-stream'` and filter/normalize legacy rows before relay publication. |
| Destructive SQLite cleanup breaks existing local databases | medium | Keep `transport` and `tmux_session_name` columns; only add comments/tests around retained compatibility. |
</threat_model>
